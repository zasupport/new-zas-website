# ZA Support UVP Quick Reference — All 18 Value Propositions

**Use this card in:** Sales calls, site visits, emails, WhatsApp, reports, proposals, renewals.

---

## THE 18 UVPs AT A GLANCE

| # | UVP | One-liner |
|---|---|---|
| 01 | POPIA Section 21 Operator Agreement | "Your practice is required by law to have a signed written agreement with any IT company that handles patient data. Ours is included from day one." |
| 02 | Medical Practice IT Specialist | "We don't also fix printers at accounting firms. Everything we do for medical practices is built around POPIA, HPCSA, and the National Health Act." |
| 03 | Dr Templates Assessment | "A 13-page compliance-focused IT assessment written for a doctor, not an IT manager. Risk to patients first, technology second." |
| 04 | Health Check Monitoring | "Every device in your practice is monitored continuously. We see problems before they cause downtime or data loss." |
| 05 | Practitioner Personal Liability | "The compliance obligation sits with you personally. Having the right IT partner helps you meet it." |
| 06 | 7-Category Risk Scoring | "We score your practice across seven areas. You see exactly where the gaps are and what to fix first." |
| 07 | Gazette 54268 (March 2026) | "New health data regulations came into force in March 2026 with no grace period. Has your IT setup been reviewed since?" |
| 08 | Predictive Maintenance | "We spot hard drives failing, batteries degrading, and certificates expiring before they cause problems." |
| 09 | Data-Backed Reporting | "When we say we maintained your security, we show you the data. Not marketing. Verifiable evidence." |
| 10 | Integrated Ecosystem | "One provider for monitoring, repairs, assessment, and compliance. One agreement. One point of accountability." |
| 11 | Breach Notification Readiness | "If something goes wrong, we detect it, notify you immediately, and help you meet the 72-hour regulatory obligation." |
| 12 | Cost Avoidance | "Our fee is measurable. The cost of a compliance failure is not. R 10 million in potential fines vs R 4,499 per month." |
| 13 | Family Extension | "For less than R 20 a day, every device in your household is covered. Patient data doesn't stop at the office door." |
| 14 | Compliance Certificate | "A formal document confirming your operator agreement is in place. File it, display it, produce it if the Regulator asks." |
| 15 | Cyber Insurance Alignment | "Signed agreements, monitoring reports, and verified backups are exactly what your insurer wants to see." |
| 16 | Sub-Operator Management | "Every third-party tool we use that touches your data has its own operator agreement with us." |
| 17 | Termination and Data Handling | "If you ever leave, your data is securely returned or destroyed and certified. No black holes." |
| 18 | Billing System Awareness | "We understand VeriClaim, ICD-10 codes, and medical aid integrations. Your billing system handles patient data too." |
